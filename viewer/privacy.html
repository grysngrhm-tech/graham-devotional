Privacy Policy

Last updated: December 4, 2024

The Graham Bible ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our website and services.

Information We Collect

When you create an account, we collect:

  • Email address — Used solely for authentication via magic link sign-in

When you use our service, we store:

  • Favorite stories — Stories you mark as favorites
  • Read status — Which stories you have read
  • Image preferences — Your selected primary images for stories

How We Use Your Information

Your information is used exclusively to:

  • Authenticate your account and provide secure access
  • Save your reading progress and preferences
  • Personalize your experience across devices

We do not use your information for marketing, advertising, or any purpose other than providing the service.

Data Storage and Security

Your data is stored securely using Supabase, a trusted cloud database provider. We implement appropriate technical measures to protect your personal information against unauthorized access, alteration, or destruction.

Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or share your personal information with third parties. Your data is used solely to provide The Graham Bible service.

Cookies and Tracking

We do not use cookies for tracking or advertising. The only data stored locally is your authentication session token, which is necessary for keeping you signed in.

Children's Privacy

The Graham Bible is designed as a family-friendly Bible devotional. However, in compliance with COPPA (Children's Online Privacy Protection Act), users must be 13 years of age or older to create an account. If you are under 13, please use The Graham Bible with a parent or guardian's supervision without creating an account.

Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your account and view your stored preferences
  • Use the service without creating an account (with limited features)

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated revision date. Continued use of the service after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

Questions

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy, please reach out through our GitHub repository at github.com/grysngrhm-tech/graham-devotional.
